Welcome to Physics Olympiad Prep!

This website is designed to help students preparing for International Physics Olympiads. Here, you can find explanations, interactive simulations, and practice problems for key physics concepts.

Mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of physics dealing with the motion of objects and the forces that affect that motion. Topics include Newton's laws, energy, momentum, and rotational dynamics.

Electromagnetism

Electromagnetism describes the interactions between electrically charged particles. Topics cover electric fields, magnetic fields, circuits, and electromagnetic waves.

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics involves studying the relationships between heat, work, temperature, and energy. Topics include the laws of thermodynamics, heat engines, and entropy.

Waves and Optics

This section covers the behavior of waves, including sound and light, and optical phenomena like reflection, refraction, and diffraction.

Modern Physics

Modern physics focuses on the behavior of matter and energy under extreme conditions or on the quantum scale. Topics include relativity, quantum mechanics, and atomic structure.
